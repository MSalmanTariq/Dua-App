Features
Dua App

Please read this before using the app!


Login:

Login:Login through Firebase Authentication service by Email and Password

Fields:All feilds are required


Signup:

Signup:Signup through Firebase Authentication service + Firebase database (for addition information of user)

Fields:All feilds are required


Home:

Submit Dua:Submit dua by entering Talib e Dua and Dua in the form

Fields:All feilds are required


Feed:

Feed:Redirect to Feed page where you can see all feeds posted by member

Like:You can any like feed

Unlike:You can unlike any post that was liked by you

Comments:You can comment any feed on the page

Comment Delete:You can delete your comment, But app does not allow you to delete someone else comment

Notification:You will be notify by icon and audio tune when someone post new feed



MyFeed:

MyFeed:Redirect to MyFeed page where you can access your posted feed, you can edit or delete your previous feeds

Edit:You can edit your post

Delete:You can delete your post
